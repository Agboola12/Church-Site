Our Church



We‘re glad and excited you‘re here. You‘re most welcome!

The True Vine Center Miami Florida, is a non-denominational, dynamic, vibrant, multicultural and multiracial congregation of the Redeemed Christian Church of God in Miami Florida, targeted towards raising disciples to impact their community and take over the world for JESUS through the power of the Holy Spirit in all spheres and strata of human endeavors.


At The True Vine Center Miami Florida, we believe a major revival and a new move of God is sweeping through American Continent and the entire world, and we‘ve been entrusted by the Lord through grace to be part of the saints to champion this move as we commit ourselves to raising kingdom army, gifted, skillful and uniquely anointed young adults, professionals, men and women with unusual mantle to fulfill their destiny in this last days.


True Vine is a church that celebrates diversity, committed to intense worship, inspiring music, undiluted word and capacity building. We are a family of love, faith and purpose. It’s a family you want to belong and it’s a family of your dream. Trust me!
