Virtual Church Purpose


“And this gospel of the kingdom shall be preached in all the world for a witness unto all nations; and then shall the end come.”
(Matt. 24: 14 KJV)


Virtual Church concept has become imperative in today’s fast moving digital and advanced technological world.


In fulfillment of divine prophecy according to Matthew 24: 14 (KJV) – “And this gospel of the kingdom shall be preached in all the world for a witness unto all nations; and then shall the end come.” Hence, we believe the church has a responsibility to take the gospel to the people wherever they are especially those who couldn’t physically go to church for different reasons. For instance, people who are in hostile or safety concern area, on the sick bed, disability, proximity, travelling, on vacation, or in a neighborhood without any church all around the world.


Virtual Church provides the opportunity for people to gather together on our various virtual platforms (Online Church Service – Live Streaming, Virtual Reality Church Service, Church Service Video-On-Demand and Social Media – Facebook live, Podcast Church Service and Teleconference/Media Church Service etc.) and still be able to enjoy and have a total church experience which includes but not limited to – Worship, Inspiring Music, Undiluted Word and Complete Fellowship, Networking and Connections amongst people of like minds.
